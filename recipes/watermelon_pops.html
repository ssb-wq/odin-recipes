Watermelon Pops

Description

These refreshing watermelon popsicles are easy to make by blending seedless watermelon with honey. Freeze in your favorite popsicle molds for a cool and fruity summertime treat!

Ingredients

  • 3/2 cups seedless watermelon, diced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend watermelon, water, honey, lemon juice, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into popsicle molds and freeze until solid, about 2 hours.
  4. Run hot water over popsicle molds for a few seconds to unmold.
